1. Core Value Proposition: The 10x Recruiter
We are not selling software. We are selling Operational Leverage. Brydg enables any organisation to handle 10x the volume of candidates without hiring 10x the staff.

The Logical Case
	•	Cost Reduction:  AI reduces Cost-per-Screen by up to 80% versus manual screening.
	•	Speed-to-Submittal:  Brydg interviews 100 people in the time it takes a human to interview one.
	•	Data-Driven Shortlisting:  Every candidate ranked against a uniform rubric. No bias. No gut feelings.
	•	24/7 Global Reach:  The AI does not sleep. No talent is ever lost to a timezone.

The Emotional Case
	•	Eliminate Screening Burnout:  Free recruiters from repetitive first-round calls to focus on high-value work.
	•	Professional Prestige:  A Brydg-Verified report makes any agency look like a tech-forward leader.
	•	Win Certainty:  Stop losing placement fees because another agency moved faster.

2. Pricing Structure
One price for everyone. Agencies, direct businesses, government. No confusion. No complex quotes. No reason to hesitate.

Platform Subscription  —  $29 / month
  •  Full access to the Brydg Dashboard
  •  Unlimited job post creations and candidate ranking
  •  Google Meet and video integration included

Pay-As-You-Go  —  $1 / completed interview
  •  Only pay for what you use
  •  No charge for no-shows or cancelled meetings
  •  Predictable costs that track directly against placement fees

Example: Bank of Kigali interviews 200 candidates in a month. Total cost: $229. Cheaper than the electricity and coffee consumed by a room of human interviewers doing the same thing.

5. Ministry Strategy: The National Infrastructure Pitch
For government ministries we do not sell a product. We sell a solution to unemployment friction. Language shifts from efficiency to fairness, meritocracy, and national transformation.

MIFOTRA: Ministry of Public Service and Labour
Angle: Digital Meritocracy
Pitch Brydg as the tool to handle Civil Service Exams and initial interviews.
It removes the possibility of human bias or nepotism. Every candidate is interviewed
and scored by the same neutral AI agent. Same questions. Same rubric. Every time.

MINICT: Ministry of ICT and Innovation
Angle: Tech-Enabled Workforce
Request integration into the Irembo portal or recognition as a standard tool for
the Digital Ambassadors programme. Brydg is a Rwandan-built AI product that
embodies the government's own digital transformation narrative.

National Employment Programme: Kora Wigire
Angle: Mass Screening at Scale
They move thousands of people through TVET vocational training each year.
Brydg acts as the exit interview to certify that graduates are job-market ready.
Every graduate screened consistently and fairly before entering the workforce.

6. Direct Business Outreach: Every Company That Is Hiring
Recruitment agencies are not the only target. Any company posting a job in the last 30 days is a warm lead. Same pricing. Same product. Different conversation.

How to Identify Direct Business Leads
	•	LinkedIn Jobs:  Filter by Kigali, Rwanda. Any active posting is a target. Check daily.
	•	JobinRwanda.com:  The dominant local job board. Every posting is a lead.
	•	BrighterMonday Rwanda:  Regional board with strong Rwanda presence.
	•	Kigali Innovation City:  Cluster of tech companies all likely hiring. One visit, multiple leads.
	•	Norrsken House Kigali:  Home to Jasiri and dozens of startups. High density of growing companies.
